Adobong Manok

Gwyn's Adobong Manok

It's sweet, mildly spicy, a hint of sourness and saltiness.

Ingredients:

  • Manok
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar (white/brown)
  • Siling Labuyo
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Vinegar
  • Cooking Oil
  • Oyster sauce
  • Pepper
  • Water

Optional:

  • Spring Onion
  1. Step 1: Dice garlic and onions.
  2. Step 2: Saute diced ingredients in 1/2 tbsp of oil.
  3. Step 3: Add in the manok.
  4. Step 4: Add 3 parts soy sauce, 2 Parts oyster sauce, 1 parts vinegar, and enough water to almost submerge the manok.
  5. Step 5: Add salt, sugar, and pepper to taste.
  6. Step 6: Let it simmer and reduce until manok is kayumangi.
  7. Step 7: Remove majority of remaining soup.
  8. Step 8: Saute manok with small amount of soup until lightly burnt.
  9. Step 9: Pour in remaining soup and let it simmer until cooked.
